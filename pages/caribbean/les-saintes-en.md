h1: 🇬🇵 Les Saintes

country-desc: |
  **[Les Saintes](https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Les_Saintes){: target="_blank"}**, a few minutes by ferry from Guadeloupe, is a little slice of paradise. Three companies serve the island regularly; from the airport you can take a taxi to Trois-Rivières and catch a ferry from there to Les Saintes.

  With around twenty sites accessible from Terre-de-Haut, the sites are rich in fixed wildlife and tropical fish, with few divers around. **Le Sec Pâté**, between Terre-de-Haut and Guadeloupe, is absolutely stunning&nbsp;!

  **<i class="fa fa-mail-forward"></i>**{: .accent-color}Here's a map and description of the dive sites of Les Saintes&nbsp;: [<i class="fa fa-map"></i>](https://www.dive-bouteille.com/copie-de-description){: target="_blank"}.

dive-shop: |
  In March 2017.
  {: .visited-in}

  **[La dive bouteille <i class="fa fa-heart"></i>](http://www.dive-bouteille.com/){: target="_blank"}** &nbsp;: Laurence and Philippe are a charming, funny and passionate couple.

  We learned a lot and passed our Level 2 with Philippe, a great teacher.

  The boat has a large capacity. It's possible to do 2 dives in the morning, leaving the afternoon free.

accommodation: |
  There are plenty of furnished rentals on Terre-de-Haut, go for the heights for a stunning view over the bay.

to-see: |
  Visit the pretty village of Terre-de-Haut and the island's beautiful beaches.

  Try a rum at the **Comptoir du nouveau monde**.

    place: Pointe Cabri
    date: 19/03/2017
    text: |
      A solo night dive, we move among the gorgonians and coral forests, crabs bury themselves in the sand as we approach. We hear whale song, at night, it's simply magical&nbsp;! On our little solo tour, we come across moray eels, snake eels out of their holes and a few spiny lobsters out too. There's a lot of plankton and visibility isn't great, but we're loving every minute.
  - author: Sam
    place: Le sec pâté
    date: 24/03/2017
    text: |
      Easy descent along the line, not much current, just a bit of swell, the reef sits at 15 meters with a magnificent wall dropping into the deep blue. Beautiful chimneys and ski-slope-like formations remind me of the Palancar site in Cozumel, it's that rich and beautiful. A multitude of fish have gathered here, a beautiful green moray, fully out of its hole, is out hunting. In the distance, big turtles are busy on the coral. Beautiful black corals between 30 and 40 meters. A gorgeous site I'd happily dive again, there's so much to see&nbsp;!
